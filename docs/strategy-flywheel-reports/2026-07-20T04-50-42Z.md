## Prompt changes

### `kalshi:paper:commander`

- Classification: `correctness_or_safety`
- File/block: `docs/agent-prompts/kalshi-prompts.proto`, trigger `commander`
- Prior behavior: the agent interpreted an unpriced zero placeholder as drawdown and said waiting could age the graduation failure out; it also behaved as if graduation paused paper trading.
- Change: current tools/prompt override old report conclusions; unpriced positions are unknown valuation; paper trading is not gated by graduation; max drawdown does not age out; trading for graduation volume is prohibited.
- Supporting evidence: the July 17 `20:00Z` / `20:05Z` / `23:05Z` snapshot discontinuity and current flat portfolio.
- Holdout evidence: not required for a data-integrity correction.
- Expected metric: no future `MAX_DRAWDOWN` failure caused solely by unpriced snapshots; no paper cycles paused solely because graduation is incomplete.
- Rollback trigger: a fully priced loss snapshot is incorrectly excluded or paper BUYs continue after a genuine configured loss stop.

### `kalshi:real:commander_real`

- Classification: `correctness_or_safety`
- File/block: `docs/agent-prompts/kalshi-prompts.proto`, trigger `commander_real`
- Prior behavior: every BUY was described and enforced as risk adding, so a bounded BUY of YES could not cover an existing negative YES position while graduation was incomplete.
- Change: the prompt and server distinguish risk-adding BUYs from exact, bounded short covers; new risk remains blocked by graduation and unpriced official positions.
- Supporting evidence: official residuals included `KXCPI-26JUL-T0.2` at `-102` net YES and `KXGDP-26JUL30-T2.0` at `+39` YES; prior SELL liquidation attempts were cancelled/unfilled.
- Holdout evidence: not required for a liquidation-path safety correction.
- Expected metric: eligible short-cover requests reach official submission while risk-adding BUYs still return the graduation gate.
- Rollback trigger: a BUY exceeding the short, using the opposite outcome, or opening fresh exposure bypasses graduation or risk validation.

### `kalshi:paper:high_freq_retro`

- Classification: `correctness_or_safety`
- File/block: `docs/agent-prompts/kalshi-prompts.proto`, trigger `high_freq_retro`
- Prior behavior: stored percentage points such as `3`, `5`, and `15` were rejected by the ratio parser and replaced by permissive defaults; the route accepted a generic 2% edge even though the prompt required 8%; the agent explicitly traded to build volume.
- Change: legacy percentage points normalize safely, the server enforces an 8% edge floor, 6 BUYs/day, and 2 BUYs/run, and the prompt forbids volume-building and treating unpriced marks as losses.
- Supporting evidence: current strategy registration plus eight same-day BUY fills and reports that explicitly cited graduation/volume as a motive.
- Holdout evidence: not required for server/prompt consistency.
- Expected metric: zero accepted BUYs below 8% claimed net edge and zero accepted BUYs beyond the configured daily/run caps.
- Rollback trigger: valid ratios in `[0,1]` change meaning, stricter user limits are loosened, or the caps count SELL risk reductions as new BUYs.

### `polymarket_us:paper:high_freq_retro`

- Classification: `correctness_or_safety`
- File/block: `docs/agent-prompts/polymarket-us-prompts.proto`, trigger `high_freq_retro`
