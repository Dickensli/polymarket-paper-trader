- Classification: `correctness_or_safety`
- File/block: `docs/agent-prompts/kalshi-prompts.proto`, trigger `commander`
- Prior behavior: graduation was described as post-decision, but the decision boundary and report completion obligation were not mechanically explicit.
- Change: added a decision checkpoint, prohibited graduation from appearing in trade/no-trade reasons, required verbatim server risk configuration, and required a truthful completion report even for incomplete coverage.
- Supporting evidence: no completed report after the `2026-07-20T22:00:10.835Z` reset; the run beginning near `22:00:12Z` remained running during the audit.

- Expected metric: fewer reportless runs and zero graduation-based paper decisions over the next completed cycles.
